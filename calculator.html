How This Calculator Works

This calculator applies the discharge bar provisions of the Bankruptcy Code:

  • Section 727(a)(8) -- 8-year bar: Chapter 7 to Chapter 7
  • Section 727(a)(9) -- 6-year bar: Chapter 13 to Chapter 7 (with exceptions)
  • Section 1328(f)(1) -- 4-year bar: Chapter 7 to Chapter 13
  • Section 1328(f)(2) -- 2-year bar: Chapter 13 to Chapter 13
  • Section 109(g) -- 180-day filing bar after certain dismissals

All periods are measured from filing date to filing date. This calculator runs entirely in your browser -- no data is sent to any server.
